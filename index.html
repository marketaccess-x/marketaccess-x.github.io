About the Platform

Intelligence built for decision-makers, not analysts

Market Access X sits at the intersection of geopolitics, supply chains, and trade policy — going deeper than commodity analysts and broader than sector specialists, to deliver intelligence that is rigorous without being inaccessible.

Coverage follows the chokepoints, not the calendar — wherever regulatory pressure, export controls, or shifting industrial geography are creating supply chain exposure that decision-makers need to understand and act on.

Follow the money, not the narrative

Understanding who benefits from a regulatory restriction is more analytically revealing than accepting the policy framing at face value.

Companies aren't passive victims

Regulatory pressure creates strategic repositioning opportunities. The firms that move early — like Lynas navigating Malaysia license risk — capture disproportionate value.

03

Chokepoints are self-defeating over time

Supply chain leverage degrades the moment it's deployed — triggering the substitution investments that erode it. The timeline is the strategic variable.
